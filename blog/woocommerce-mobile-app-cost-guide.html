How Much Does a WooCommerce Mobile App Cost in 2026?

By Piyush Ahuja March 2026 8 min read

Thinking about getting a mobile app for your WooCommerce store? The cost varies dramatically depending on the approach you choose. In this guide, I'll break down every option — from free app builders to custom-coded native apps — so you can make an informed decision.

Quick Cost Overview

Approach               | Upfront Cost    | Monthly Cost  | Quality
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
DIY App Builders       | $0-100          | $50-300/mo    | Low-Medium
Template-Based Apps    | $500-2,000      | $30-100/mo    | Medium
Freelance Developer    | $1,000-10,000   | $0            | High
Development Agency     | $15,000-80,000  | $0-2,000/mo   | High
Custom (My Service)    | Starting $500   | $0            | High

Option 1: DIY App Builders ($50-300/month)

Platforms like AppMaker, MobiLoud, and Starter allow you to create a WooCommerce app without coding.

Pros:

  • Launch quickly (hours to days)
  • No technical skills required
  • Basic WooCommerce sync built-in

Cons:

  • Very limited customization
  • Ongoing monthly fees add up ($600-3,600/year)
  • Generic look and feel
  • Performance often mediocre
  • You don't own the code
  • Vendor lock-in — switching is painful

Best for: Small stores testing if mobile works for their audience before investing in custom development.

Option 2: Template-Based Development ($500-2,000)

Some developers use pre-built app templates (like from CodeCanyon) and customize them for your store.

Pros:

  • Cheaper than fully custom
  • Faster to deliver (1-2 weeks)
  • Some customization possible

Cons:

  • Limited to the template's architecture
  • Often buggy with updates
  • Many stores sharing the same template
  • Adding unique features is difficult or impossible

Option 3: Custom-Coded App from a Freelancer ($1,000-10,000)

A skilled freelance developer builds your app from scratch using React Native, Flutter, or native code.

Pros:

  • Fully tailored to your needs
  • You own the code completely
  • Direct communication with developer
  • More affordable than agencies
  • High-quality, optimized code

Cons:

  • Higher upfront cost than templates
  • Takes 2-4 weeks to build
  • Quality depends on the developer (choose carefully!)

This is what I offer. I build custom-coded React Native apps that connect to your WooCommerce backend, with full feature parity and premium UX.

Option 4: Development Agency ($15,000-80,000+)

Large agencies with teams of designers, developers, and project managers.

Pros:

  • Full-service experience
  • Large team, diverse expertise
  • Post-launch support often included

Cons:

  • Extremely expensive
  • Longer timelines (2-6 months)
  • Communication through project managers, not developers
  • Much of your budget goes to overhead, not development

What Factors Affect the Cost?

  • Number of features: Basic browse + cart vs. full-featured app with loyalty programs
  • Design complexity: Standard UI vs. custom animations and unique layouts
  • Payment integrations: Number and complexity of payment gateways
  • Push notifications: Basic or advanced segmented campaigns
  • Multi-language/currency: Additional complexity for international stores
  • Platform: iOS only, Android only, or both
