# 🌍 HOW TO GET INTERNATIONAL CLIENTS ON FIVERR (Stop Getting Only Indian Buyers)

This is a step-by-step guide to optimize your Fiverr profile and gigs to attract clients from the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and Europe — not just India.

---

## ⚠️ WHY YOU ONLY GET INDIAN CLIENTS

1. **Your pricing is too low** — US/UK clients associate low price with low quality. They skip $5-50 gigs.
2. **Your gig titles use generic keywords** — Indian freelancers flood these keywords, so Fiverr shows you to Indian buyers.
3. **No timezone/availability info** — Western clients want to know you can communicate during their business hours.
4. **Generic profile** — No mention of serving international clients or working with Western businesses.
5. **Fiverr's algorithm** — It matches sellers to buyers from similar regions unless your profile signals global service.

### 2. RAISE YOUR PRICES (THIS IS THE #1 FIX)

**Current problem:** If your starting price is $50-150, you attract budget buyers (mostly from India, Bangladesh, Pakistan).

**Price strategy for international clients:**

| Package | Current (DON'T) | New Price (DO) |
|---------|-----------------|----------------|
| Basic | $50-150 | $300-500 |
| Standard | $150-300 | $500-1,000 |
| Premium | $300-500 | $1,000-2,500 |

**Why this works:**
- US/UK clients have budgets of $500-5,000 for custom development
- They SKIP gigs under $200 because they assume it's low quality
- Higher price = Fiverr shows you to buyers with bigger budgets
- You get fewer orders but MUCH more revenue per order
- Less work, more money, better clients

**IMPORTANT:** Raise prices gradually over 2 weeks, not all at once.

## 🎯 FIVERR ALGORITHM HACKS FOR INTERNATIONAL VISIBILITY

### 1. Respond to messages within 1 hour
Fiverr rewards fast response time with higher search ranking.

### 2. Keep your gig active
Log in daily, respond to any message instantly. Fiverr demotes inactive sellers.

### 3. Get your first US/UK reviews
- Lower your price temporarily for 2-3 orders to get international reviews
- Reviews from US/UK buyers make Fiverr show you to more US/UK buyers
- After getting reviews, raise prices back up

### 4. Use Fiverr's "Availability" feature
- Set your availability to overlap with US business hours (9 AM - 6 PM EST)
- This makes you appear active when US buyers are searching

### 5. Language test
- Take the Fiverr English Language Test (score 9.0+)
- This appears on your profile and signals professionalism

### 6. Fiverr SEO
- Your gig URL includes your title — use keywords US buyers search for
- "headless woocommerce nextjs" not "woocommerce website cheap"
